Cuttle Bot

How can complex strategy games be taught? How can tools designed to help beginners approach complex games balance the learner's need to master the rules against their need to develop a sense of strategy?

Cuttle Bot allows players to practice the world's greatest card game against an educational AI that teaches them the rules and how to approach the game's strategy.

Cuttle Bot is a web application built in Angular 6 that implements a customized version of the MiniMax algorithm to make its strategic decisions. Players can use the reverse button in the upper-left to undo their move(s) and see how the game plays out when they make different choices.

CuttleBot provides mixed initiative, just-in-time feedback to detect when players fail to win or avoid a preventable loss, and nonjudgmentally alerts players of opportunities for better moves. Players learn the ropes through guided discovery; they are free to play their own way and also shown critical details with scaffolding hints.
